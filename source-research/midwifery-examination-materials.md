# Public Midwifery Examination Materials

Research date: 2026-05-02

## Scope

This report summarizes publicly available midwifery examination and assessment materials, with priority on practical skills assessments such as OSCE-style stations that include rubrics, step-by-step clinical procedure descriptions, and marking criteria.

Secondary targets include written MCQ exams, CBT practice materials, and clinical reasoning questions with explanations.

## Summary

The strongest public sources are:

1. **ICM Essential Competencies - Assessment Guide**: most reusable assessment-design content; includes OSCE examples, rubric levels, and performance criteria. CC BY-NC-SA 4.0 — best RAG candidate from this set.
2. **UK NMC Midwifery Test of Competence OSCE materials**: strongest publicly available practical OSCE/rubric material; NMC copyrighted — permission needed for RAG ingestion.
3. **Jhpiego ReproLinePlus clinical skills checklists**: high clinical value for procedures (PPH, normal birth, newborn care); permission from Jhpiego needed for RAG ingestion.
4. **Malta / Mater Dei Hospital Guide for Midwifery Skills**: detailed labour and birth procedures; strict Mater Dei Hospital copyright — permission required.

## Strongest Candidates for Ingestion or Manual Review

### 1. ICM Essential Competencies - Assessment Guide

- Most reusable assessment-design source found; CC BY-NC-SA 4.0 — best openly licensed candidate.
- Useful for assessment framework, competency mapping, and rubric design; not a station-level procedure bank.

### 2. UK NMC Midwifery OSCE Marking Criteria and Mock OSCE

- Most detailed practical midwifery assessment material found publicly.
- Contains station-level marking criteria and scenario expectations — strong fit for procedure-oriented retrieval.
- NMC copyrighted; requires permission before RAG ingestion.

### 3. Jhpiego ReproLinePlus Clinical Skills Checklists

- High clinical value: step-by-step procedure checklists for PPH management, normal birth, newborn care, and family planning.
- Directly relevant to low-resource settings. Requires confirming permission with Jhpiego before ingestion.

### 4. Malta / Mater Dei Hospital Guide for Midwifery Skills

- Detailed practical guide covering labour assessment, ARM, fetal monitoring, cord prolapse, and neonatal examination.
- Authored by Mater Dei Hospital (Malta) midwifery staff; strict institutional copyright — Mater Dei Hospital permission required.

### 5. Australia NMBA / Ahpra IQNM Materials

- Official candidate materials for internationally qualified midwives.
- Better for exam structure and documentation than for detailed clinical rubric content.
