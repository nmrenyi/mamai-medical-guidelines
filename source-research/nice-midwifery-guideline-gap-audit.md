# NICE Midwifery and Obstetric Guideline Gap Audit

Research date: 2026-04-30

## Scope

This audit searched the NICE website (`nice.org.uk`) for guidance relevant to midwifery, maternity, obstetric, reproductive health and newborn care that is not yet represented in the current MAMAI NICE corpus.

The existing corpus contains these NICE sources:

- Abortion care (2019)
- Contraception (2014)
- Domestic violence and abuse / GBV (2014)
- Antenatal care (2021)
- Antenatal and postnatal mental health (2020)
- Vitamin D and nutrition in pregnancy (2025)
- Intrapartum care (2023)
- Postnatal care (2021)

Primary NICE pages checked:

- [Women's and reproductive health guidelines hub](https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/indevelopment/gid-hub10001)
- [Pregnancy topic page](https://www.nice.org.uk/Guidance/Conditions-and-diseases/Fertility--pregnancy-and-childbirth/Pregnancy)
- NICE guideline pages for pregnancy, intrapartum care, neonatal care, sepsis, and related HealthTech/diagnostics guidance.

## Existing NICE Corpus Items That Need Refresh Checks

| Existing corpus item | Current NICE status on 2026-04-30 | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Abortion care | NICE NG140 was published in 2019 and is now last updated 27 May 2025. The anti-D prophylaxis recommendations were updated to refer to WHO guidance; minor HIV testing clarification was added in February 2025; early medical abortion legislation update was added in 2022. | Refresh the corpus PDF. |
| Intrapartum care | NICE NG235 was published in 2023 and is now last updated 14 November 2025. NICE made new and updated recommendations on labouring in water and water birth. | Refresh the corpus PDF. |
| Postnatal care | NICE NG194 was published in 2021. NICE lists minor link/content maintenance through November 2025, including links to the new suspected sepsis in pregnant or recently pregnant people guideline. | Refresh if preserving current cross-links and sepsis references matters. |
| Maternal and child nutrition / vitamin D and nutrition in pregnancy | NICE NG247 was published 15 January 2025. Minor wording changes were made in 2025, including clarifications around BMI thresholds and gestational diabetes risk. | Likely current if the corpus used the latest 2025 PDF; otherwise refresh. |
| Domestic violence and abuse / GBV | NICE PH50 was published in 2014. NICE reviewed it on 11 July 2024 and plans a full update, but no final replacement guideline was found yet. | Track update; no replacement to ingest yet. |
| Antenatal care | NICE NG201 was published in 2021. NICE reviewed it on 27 December 2024 and added a link to end-of-life care guidance; no major clinical update found. | No urgent refresh unless exact current link set is required. |
| Antenatal and postnatal mental health | NICE CG192 was last formally updated 11 February 2020. Later update notes link to MHRA safety advice on valproate and antidepressants, but no replacement guideline was found. | No major replacement identified. |
| Contraception (2014) | If this refers to NICE PH51, it remains a 2014 public health guideline. NICE also has Long-acting reversible contraception CG30, last updated 2019, which is not the same source and may be worth adding separately. | Consider adding CG30 if postpartum or long-acting contraceptive counselling depth matters. |

## Notes On Prioritisation

The strongest immediate additions are NG126, NG133, NG3, NG25, NG137, NG229, NG192, NG207, NG121, NG255, NG195, CG98 and NG124. Together these cover the requested priority areas:

- ectopic pregnancy and miscarriage: NG126
- hypertension and pre-eclampsia: NG133 and HTG630
- gestational diabetes: NG3
- preterm labour and birth: NG25 and NG124
- multiple pregnancy: NG137
- neonatal care: NG195, CG98 and NG124
- sepsis in obstetrics: NG255 and NG121
- placental complications: NG192, NG133 and HTG630

I did not find a standalone NICE clinical guideline dedicated to stillbirth or reduced fetal movements. Relevant NICE coverage is distributed across fetal monitoring in labour, antenatal and postnatal mental health, high-risk intrapartum care, preterm labour and neonatal guidance.
